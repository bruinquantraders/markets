Colonel Blotto

Deploy 100 troops across 10 fields.

Win a field by sending more troops than your opponent. You play every other strategy head-to-head; your rank is the total ground you take across the board. Enter a username to start — return anytime to refine your deployment.

No password — your username loads your saved deployment.

How scoring works
  • Allocate exactly 100 troops over 10 fields (0–100 each).
  • Against each opponent, you win a field if you place strictly more troops there; a tie splits it (0.5 each).
  • You face every submitted strategy once (round-robin). Your score is total field points won; ranking is by score.
  • Resubmitting overwrites your previous deployment.
